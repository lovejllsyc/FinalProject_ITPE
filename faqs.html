Frequently Asked Questions

1. Are your basketball products original?

Yes! We resell authentic products from trusted brands like Nike, Adidas, and Spalding. Quality and authenticity are our top priorities.

2. Why are your prices lower than other stores?

We source directly from suppliers and focus on high-volume selling, allowing us to offer premium products at more affordable prices.

3. What products do you offer?

We offer basketball shoes, jerseys, shorts, balls, warmers, socks, and accessories—all from top brands.

4. Are your products good for professional or competitive play?

Absolutely! Our products meet performance standards and are suitable for training, casual games, and competitive play.

5. Do you have different sizes available?

Yes, we offer a wide range of sizes for both men and women. Size charts are available to help you choose the perfect fit.

6. How can I be sure about the quality?

We carefully inspect all items before selling and only work with reliable suppliers to ensure durability and performance.

7. Do you offer discounts or promos?

Yes! We regularly offer discounts, bundle deals, and seasonal promos—perfect for teams and groups.

8. Can I order in bulk for a team?

Definitely! We accept bulk orders and can offer special pricing for teams, schools, or organizations.

9. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment options including GCash, bank transfer, and cash on delivery (depending on location).

10. How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 2–5 days depending on your location. We also provide tracking for your convenience.

11. Do you accept returns or exchanges?

Yes, we accept returns or exchanges for defective or incorrect items within a specified period.

12. Are your basketballs suitable for outdoor use?

Yes, we offer both indoor and outdoor basketballs designed for durability and grip on different surfaces.

13. Do you restock sold-out items?

Yes, popular items are restocked regularly. You can message us to check availability or reserve items.

14. Can I see actual photos of the products?

Of course! We provide real photos upon request so you can see exactly what you're getting.

15. Why should I choose your store?

We combine trusted brands, affordable prices, and reliable service—making us your go-to shop for basketball needs.
